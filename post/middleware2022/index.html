Current Conference: MIDDLEWARE 2022

Mar 10, 2022

MIDDLEWARE 2022 will be held in Quebec City, Canada.

General Chairs: Kaiwen Zhang and Abdelouahed Gherbi, ETS Montreal, Canada and Paolo Bellavista, University of Bologna, Italy.

Program Chairs: Saurabh Bagchi, Purdue University, USA and Marta Patino-Martinez, Polytechnic University of Madrid, Spain.

We are currently planning for a physical conference in Quebec City, scheduled for November 2022. Due to the current pandemic, our plans may change and require a virtual conference. We will provide updates as they become available through our various channels (Website, Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn).
